Sentr.io

Simple Two-Step Authentication.

No More Passwords.

When you visit a service using Sentr.io, you just enter your username or email address and your phone gets a notification you can either approve or deny.

One Click Sign Up.

If you'd like to sign up for a service using Sentr.io just hit the sign up button and approve it on your phone.

A Single Profile To Rule Them All.

Once you've filled out your details on Sentr.io, they'll be shared with any other service you use it with. No more updating dozens of profiles when your information changes!
